Welcome To Thomson & Joseph Ltd

 
 
 

Thomson & Joseph Ltd was established in 1951 as a supplier of pharmaceutical raw materials to the developing UK pharmaceutical industry following the end of World War 2 by Colin Thomson and Ernest Joseph, who were brothers-in-law. Thomson was a GP, and Joseph a pharmaceutical chemist. Both were fluent German speakers and early supplies of pharmaceutical raw materials were sourced from Germany. The company was originally sited in Baker Street, London and moved to Norwich during the 1960s.

Thomson & Joseph Ltd has been accredited to ISO9001:2015 Quality Standard since 1994. We have, over the years, developed strong relationships with key producers including:

  • Orrion Chemicals Metalchem (OCM) - a producer of metal nitrates, high purity metal oxides and a wide range of Bismuth salts.
     
  • 5N Plus - a leading producer of specialty metal and chemical products. 5N Plus manufactures critical precursors and key enablers used in a number of industries such as clean energy, electronic, pharmaceutical, medical imaging, paint pigments, security and surveillance. 
     
  • Albion Advanced Nutrition - a producer of high quality foliar metalosates for human and plant nutrition.
  • Landus Cooperative - inventor of SoyChlor, designed to assist pre-freshening dairy cows with DCAB balancing and reducing instances of sub clinical hypocalcemia and, in turn, milk fever. SoyChlor's unique ability to deliver chloride while maintaining palatability has made it the preferred feed ingredient by dairy cows across the globe.
     
  • BioAg Australia - based in Narrandera NSW Australia, they manufacture solid natural phosphate-based fertilisers, efficiency and yield enhancing liquid microbial fermented cultures, and effluent digesters which are supplied in tailored programmes to suit individual farm or grower requirements.

In essence, T&J’s rationale is to represent overseas chemical manufacturers operating in niche markets as their sales agent and distributor in the UK and Ireland. Today T&J has a range of agencies, as shown below, which cover the pharmaceutical, industrial and agricultural industries.

1. Pharmaceutical:
5N Plus, Germany – Bismuth salts. (G.I. tract medicines)

2. Industrial:
OCM, France – Nitrate salts: Nickel, Aluminium, Copper, Cobalt, Manganese, Zinc, Iron and Ferric salts (catalysts and electronics).
TIB Chemicals, Germany – Nickel salts (Electroplating).
5N Plus, Germany – Bismuth salts as a replacement for lead in ceramics, lubricants and electronics.

3. Agricultural:
Albion Plant Nutrition, USA – Metal Amino Acid Chelates (plants).
Landus Coorperative, USA - Soychlor – a dietetic feedingstuff for pre-calving cows.
BioAg, Australia – Biological nutrients for soil and plants.

4. Analytical:
Albion Laboratory Services
Mineral analysis support for the use of agricultural products, using the analytical capabilities of NRM, Sciantec and Sci-Tech Labs.

We are always seeking new partnerships and product areas to develop our business.

 
MISSION STATEMENT

It is the policy of Thomson & Joseph Limited to provide our customers with quality products supported by an efficient, reliable service.

This is implemented by a quality programme which is outlined in the Quality Manual.
It is the established policy of the company to provide goods and services which are compliant with customer and market requirements.

We will accomplish this by:

• Establishing strategic partnerships that will produce significant revenue, enhance our reputation and increase our technology base.

• Marshalling the necessary resources across the company to develop flexibility and responsiveness to meet changing customer needs.

• Providing a friendly and polite response to customer requests and queries.

• Ensuring all staff are aware that it is their responsibility to provide a quality service.

• Maintaining and continually  reviewing and revising a Quality Assurance System to develop and improve our services.

Dr. D. Atherton
Managing Director
July 2014
